About

BookVerse is a user-friendly website designed for book lovers. It allows users to search for books by title, author, or keyword, and presents them with a list of related books. Users can access detailed information about each book, including reviews from other BookVerse members. Additionally, users can create an account to build a personalized bookshelf, where they can add books they want to read and remove them once finished. Having an account also enables users to write reviews for the books they've read. BookVerse is the perfect destination for avid readers seeking a convenient and interactive platform to explore, organize, and engage with their favorite books.

F&Q

To have access to the bookshelf feature, you'll need to have an account and be logged in on our website. A bookshelf feature provides you an easy way to keep track of the titles that capture your attention. You can add books to your bookshelf and easily view the list whenever you visit our site. Once you've read a book, you have the option to remove it from your bookshelf, ensuring your list stays updated and organized.

To write a review on our website, you'll need to have an account and be logged in on our website. Once logged in, find the book you want to review. Visit the book's details page, where you'll find a text box provided for writing your review located under the details sections for that book. Express your honest thoughts and opinions about the book in the text box. When you're satisfied with your review, submit it. Congratulations! Your review will now be visible to all visitors on the webpage including those who don't have an account nor are logged in. Simply visit the book's details page to explore the existing reviews.

All the information displayed in this website comes from the Google Books API.

On the book details page, you'll find a part that tells you if that book is available for rent or to buy on Google Play, if yes, you will also have access to the price listed and a link to buy on Google Play.
